## Role

Use as a restrained orange directional/accent decoration.

## Visual Structure

- Frame about `129 x 128`.
- Union/compound shape in Orange `#F26419`.
- Intended as a small accent, not a content carrier.

## When To Use

- Warning, contrast, risk, or "do not" sections.
- A small orange accent is needed to balance an otherwise blue/purple design.
- A graphic needs variety from dot grids and pale ellipses.

## Editing Notes

Orange is semantic. Use this only when the content benefits from a warning/risk/accent cue. Keep it outside readable text areas and behind or beside the main content.

## Reject When

- The graphic is purely neutral/positive and orange would add false warning semantics.
- The shape becomes a decorative blob with no relationship to the concept.
- It is enlarged enough to dominate the composition.
