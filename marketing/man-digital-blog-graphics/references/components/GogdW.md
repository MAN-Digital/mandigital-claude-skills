## Role

Use as a compact dual-tone accent when a composition needs a small directional or energetic mark.

## Visual Structure

- Group about `147 x 165`.
- Orange ellipse/circle plus a blue path.
- More expressive than a dot grid, so it should stay small.

## When To Use

- A light-background graphic needs a small accent in a corner or near a section transition.
- A design needs orange/blue balance without changing the main card system.
- The concept has movement, transition, or emphasis.

## Editing Notes

Place at layer 0 or behind low-priority content only. Use at low opacity if it starts competing with cards or labels. Prefer this over inventing new circle-and-line motifs.

## Reject When

- It conflicts with connector direction.
- It reads as an unlabeled process node.
- It becomes one more visual system in an already dense graphic.
