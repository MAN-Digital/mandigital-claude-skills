# acPUD - nRPmP Title Block

Source parent: `nRPmP`

Observed node: `Frame 633603`, centered vertical title/subtitle stack.

## Role

Use for a strong dark-background MAN Digital poster headline. It introduces the graphic before the system diagram.

## Visual Structure

- Width about 730 px.
- Vertical auto layout with about 24 px gap.
- Title: Montserrat, 63 px, bold, white, centered.
- Subtitle: Lato, 24 px, white, centered, 1.4 line height.

## Use Cases

- Blog lead graphics.
- LinkedIn 4:5 thesis graphics.
- Dark blue explainer posters with one dominant idea.

## Editing Notes

Keep title copy short and let the subtitle do the explanation. Avoid long line lengths that make the title block feel like a paragraph.
