# xOLUX - Blue Geometric Dot Pattern

Source: Gemini carousel registry (`/Users/romeoman/.agents/skills/carousel/component-registry.md`). Current live lookup note: this node was not found in the active Marketing `Playground.pen` path during the 2026-05-28 Pencil MCP check, so verify availability before copying.

## Role

Use as a compact brand accent pattern, not as the main visual.

## Visual Structure

- Frame about `223 x 109`.
- Geometric/patterned mark in a deep blue family, documented as `#333FD0` in the carousel registry.
- Works as a small layer-0 or edge accent.

## When To Use

- Need a small structured accent near a title, footer, or open corner.
- Need variation from pale circle/ellipse decoration.
- Need a sharper digital/technical texture in a CRM, automation, or systems graphic.

## Editing Notes

Keep it away from body copy and connectors. Use sparingly on light backgrounds. Do not use it as a cover-slide decoration or large surface fill; the carousel builder notes warn that striped/pattern decorations on covers can look busy.

## Reject When

- It competes with a diagram's connector lanes.
- It appears behind small text or icon labels.
- It makes a minimal blog graphic feel decorative rather than useful.
